From a complex architecture to an award-winning story. I’m proud to announce a different kind of win from this weekend's AIxCYBER 2025 Hackathon.


Our team took home the Best Pitch Award (3rd Place), and it’s a recognition of our vision for a smarter, more accessible investment landscape in Tunisia.
The Problem: High-quality investment opportunities—vetted startups, solid companies, and prime real estate—are typically locked within the trusted walls of banks, inaccessible to the average investor. How do you democratize access without losing security?
Our Solution: VeriVest. We designed a platform that partners with banks. Banks provide the secure pipeline of vetted opportunities, and VeriVest provides the AI-powered intelligence to make those opportunities accessible to everyone.
As team leader, my focus was on building the core user experience and the "AI brain" of the platform. My contributions involved:
•Designing and developing the entire UI/UX in Next.js, creating an intuitive interface that makes sophisticated investing feel simple.
•Engineering the entire AI engine using n8n, deploying a suite of intelligent agents with Gemini and Perplexity.
• A key feature was a specialized AI valuation agent I built, which actively searches Tunisian sources like Tayara.tn , Facebook Marketplace, Reddit and other sources to generate real-time, data-driven price estimates for the real estate assets before they are tokenized on Hedera.
Building the tech was one challenge. But communicating how we seamlessly bridge the trust of traditional banking with the power of AI and the accessibility of blockchain was the real test. Winning the Best Pitch Award is a massive validation of our ability to tell that story clearly and persuasively.
This victory was a true team effort, and I was privileged to lead such a dedicated and talented group:
